# InjectSills.jl
This collects analytical solutions that gives the displacement around sills or dikes embedded in an elastic halfspace.
This can be combined with other codes such as the [GeophysicalModelGenerator](https://github.com/JuliaGeodynamics/GeophysicalModelGenerator.jl) to plot the surface displacement caused by the injection of a dike.
It can also be combined with [JustPIC](https://github.com/JuliaGeodynamics/JustPIC.jl) to advect particles after a sill or dike injection event.
